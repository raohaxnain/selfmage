What is SelfImage

SelfImage as we know it today was first discovered by Dr. Maxwell Maltz who was involved with reconstructive surgery, commonly referred to as a plastic surgery. He found that when he did surgery on a patient and possibly removed or repaired some form of disfiguration to the persons face, there was not only a great change to the persons face, there was a noticeable psychological improvement.

About Sarahjane

What is it that makes some people so confident, so successful? Is it luck? Is it genetics? Up until 10 years ago the general consensus would have been yes but breakthroughs in current day science and phycology has shown this not to be the case. We have come to learn that everyone has two images. One image is the physical image that we portray to the world, the other image is our internal image which is how we feel emotionally about ourselves. It is only when the internal image is transformed that the outer image is transformed also. At SelfImage we offer the full package of not only transforming our outer image with makeup, fashion, hair etc. but also transforming your inner self-image giving you the confidence you require to become the successful person you were always meant to be!

Founder

With over 10 years of experience in the beauty and fashion industries, from beginning her career as a hairdresser / makeup artist and progressing to fashion styling and retail management, Sarahjane has always had a passion for the education and one’s personal appearance. What makes her stand out in the industry today is the focus and passion she has for developing personal relationships with her customers, which allows her to help transform their self-image both internally and externally. Her positive mental attitude, attention to detail and infectious energy will give you the confidence and belief you require to take on any challenge coming your way in the hectic world we live in today!
